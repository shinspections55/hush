How the Silent Draft Works

Hush introduces a modern twist on fantasy football drafting with our Silent Auction Draft — a strategic, data-driven alternative to traditional snake drafts and fast-paced live auctions.

Instead of bidding out loud, every manager submits private, hidden bids on players each round. When the round ends, all bids are revealed at once, and players are awarded to the highest bidders.

Example

Let’s say Bijan Robinson is available.

Manager A bids:
$49

Manager B bids:
$55

Manager C bids:
$45

When the round ends:

  • Manager B wins Bijan Robinson for $50 because the second highest bid was $49.
  • The other managers keep their remaining budget.
  • This can also get crazy. For example, if nobody else bids on Ceedee Lamb for whatever reason, the team that bid will win him for a dollar.

No one knows the market until the reveal.

Draft Basics

Each manager begins with a set salary-cap budget.

During every round:

  • Player Pool — A group of players becomes available for bidding.
  • Secret Bidding — Managers privately submit bids on any players they want.
  • Hidden Information — No one can see what others are bidding.
  • Round Reveal — When the timer expires (or all bids are submitted), winners are revealed.
  • Winning Logic — The highest bidder wins the player for $1 more than the second-highest bid.
  • Tie Resolution — If two managers submit the same top bid, a quick live tiebreaker auction determines the winner.

Why It’s Different

Traditional drafts often reward:

  • Reaction Speed
  • Draft Position Luck
  • Fast Clicking

Silent Drafting rewards:

  • Preparation
  • Player Rankings
  • Value Recognition
  • Predicting League Behavior

Tiers & Rankings

Managers can build their own personal rankings with a drag and drop rankings creator and personalized tiers before the draft.

These rankings are also printable, so you can use them for your other drafts.

This helps identify:

  • positional drop-offs
  • value cliffs
  • sleeper targets
  • roster priorities

As players are drafted, tier scarcity becomes increasingly important.

Player Status Indicators

During the draft, player rankings update live:

  • Yellow highlight = Current round player pool
  • Green highlight = Player is on your roster
  • Red strikethrough = Drafted by another team
  • Dark faded strikethrough = Went Undrafted

This creates a dynamic draft board that constantly reflects the league market.

One of the biggest advantages: You can land players you’d never have a chance at in a snake draft due to draft position.

Every bid becomes a strategic decision, not a race against the clock.
